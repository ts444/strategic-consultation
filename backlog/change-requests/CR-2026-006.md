# CR-2026-006: Mapping phase — require explicit resolution of open assumptions before ratification

**Originating engagement:** Meridian-Logistics
**Observed in phase:** 03-mapping

## Problem

ASM-002 (MSP delivery capacity — the assumption that the MSP is resourced to deliver security controls beyond internal IT team basics) was logged at intake with `requires_revalidation: true` targeting phase 03-mapping for resolution. The mapping phase ratified service-map.md and recommendations.md without producing a formal ASM-002 resolution entry in _assumptions.md.

At engagement close, ASM-002 remains technically open (the _assumptions.md register still shows no validation status entry for ASM-002). This is a process gap: the mapping phase HITL Gate 1 checklist did not include a specific item requiring the consultant to confirm that all "requires_revalidation" assumptions targeting this phase have been resolved.

## Proposed change

1. Add a checklist item to the 03-mapping phase HITL Gate 1 pre-flight prompt: "All open assumptions with requires_revalidation targeting this phase have been resolved (or explicitly deferred with reason) in _assumptions.md."
2. Update `templates/service-map.template.md` to include an "Open Assumptions Resolution" section that lists all unresolved assumptions targeting this phase and requires the synthesizer to either resolve them or explicitly carry them forward with justification.
3. Consider generalising the checklist item to all phases that may be the target of a `requires_revalidation` flag.

## Expected improvement

Assumptions flagged for revalidation in a specific phase are formally resolved (or explicitly deferred with reason) before that phase ratifies. The _assumptions.md register is complete and accurate at engagement close, and downstream phases can rely on it as a single source of truth for assumption status.
